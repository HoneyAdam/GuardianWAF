GuardianWAF has been deployed!

Get the WAF service URL:
  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl port-forward $POD_NAME 8088:8088

{{- if .Values.dashboard.enabled }}

Access the dashboard:
  kubectl port-forward $POD_NAME 9443:9443
  Open http://localhost:9443 in your browser.
{{- end }}

Check health:
  kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}"
  kubectl logs -l "app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" --tail=20
